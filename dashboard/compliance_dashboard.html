How it works

Three steps from model to compliance report, fully automated.

1

Describe your system

Enter your model name (or API key for proprietary models), your use case, and provider details for Annex IV.

2

Glassbox runs the audit

Attribution patching identifies the causal circuit. Sufficiency, comprehensiveness, and F1 scores are computed against EU Article 13 thresholds.

3

Report generated

All 9 Annex IV sections are populated, risk flags identified, and explainability grade A–D assigned per the regulation.

4

Download & submit

Download your PDF and JSON report. Fill in the [PROVIDER TO COMPLETE] fields and submit to your national competent authority.
